    \chapter*{Lay Abstract}
    Several models of computability were previously proposed for partial functions over the reals. Some of these models were proved to be equivalent for functions satisfying specific conditions we call ``acceptability''.
    In this thesis, we prove that at least the class of elementary functions satisfies this ``acceptability'' condition. This shows that the acceptability conditions are sufficiently general.

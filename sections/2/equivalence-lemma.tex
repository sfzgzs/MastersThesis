Now, let us review the equivalence lemma proved by \citet{ModelOfCompForPartFunc_MingQuanFuAndJeffZucker}:
\begin{theorem}[Fu and Zucker's Equivalence Theorem]
\label{lemma::equivalence_alpha_tracking_wcc_appx}
For any \acceptable{} function $f:\Reals\to\Reals$ and any \effectiveopenexhaustion{} $X$ of $\dom(f)$, the following are equivalent:
\begin{itemize}
    \item $f$ is an \alphacomputablefunction{}.
    \item $f$ is \WhileCC-approximable.
    \item $f$ is \GLcomputable{} \footnote{The definition of \GrzegorczykLacomecomputability{} (GL-computability) for total functions is given in \cite[page 25]{ComputabilityInAnalysisAndPhysics_Poure-El_Richards} and later extended in \citet{ModelOfCompForPartFunc_MingQuanFuAndJeffZucker} to \emph{Grzegorczyk-Lacome computability (GL-computability) with respect to some open exhaustion} for partial functions.} w.r.t. $X$.
    \item $f$ is effectively locally uniformly multipolynomially approximable{} w.r.t. $X$.
\end{itemize}
\end{theorem}

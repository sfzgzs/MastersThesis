
\subsection{Some Basic Functions}
\label{sec::some-basic-functions}
Using $\realTimes$ we can define the procedure $\realtoNatPow : \real \times \nat \to \real$ as:

\[
    \mathsf{pow} \equiv  \\
        \BLOCK{
        \PROC \\
            \INDENT{
            \IN a:\real\ n:\nat \\
            \AUX  \counter:\nat\ r:\real }\\
        \BEGIN \\
            \INDENT{
            r := \realOne \\
            \counter := \natZero \\
        
            \WHILE \counter \natLess n \DO \\
                \INDENT{
                r := r \times a   \\
                \counter := \counter + 1 }\\
            \OD \\
            \RETURN r }\\
        \END}\\
\]

This lets us use $x^n$ as a shorthand for $\realtoNatPow(x,n)$ and $x^{-n}$ for $\realInv (\realtoNatPow(x,n))$ with $x$ being of type $\real$ and $n$ of type $\nat$.\\
We can also define the \WhileCC-procedure $\factorial : \nat \to \nat$ as below:
\[
   \mathsf{Factorial} \equiv \\
                \BLOCK{
                \PROC \\
                    \INDENT{
                    \IN n:\nat \\
                    \AUX \counter:\nat\ \res:\nat}\\
                \BEGIN \\
                    \INDENT{
                    \res := \natOne \\
                    \counter := \natOne \\
                    \WHILE \counter \natLess n + 1 \DO \\
                        \INDENT{
                        \res := \res \times \counter   \\
                        \counter := \counter + 1}\\
                    \OD \\
                    \RETURN \res }\\
                \END}\\
\]

\begin{proposition}
    We can add the operator $\mathsf{abs}$ defined as
    \begin{equation*}
    \mathsf{abs}(x) \simeq
        \begin{cases*}
            x & if $x > 0$\\
            -x & if $x < 0$\\
            \uparrow & if $x=0$\\
        \end{cases*}
    \end{equation*}
    to our algebra without strengthening it since it can easily be computed using:
    \[
        \mathsf{abs} \equiv  \\
        \BLOCK{
        \PROC \\
            \INDENT{
            \IN x:\real \\
            \AUX r:\real}\\
        \BEGIN \\
            \INDENT{
            \IF 0 \realLess x \THEN \\
                \INDENT{
                r := x}\\
            \ELSE \\
                \INDENT{
                r := -x}\\
            \FI \\
            \RETURN r}\\
        \END}\\
    \]
\end{proposition}
